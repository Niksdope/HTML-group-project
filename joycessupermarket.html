Our Products & Services

Homestore


The 4,000 sq ft Athenry Homestore which is attached to the existing supermarket opened on the 17th May 2013. The store stocks everything anyone needs to complete the look of their home with brands such as Cole and Mason and Sabichi. Among the many products are items suitable for kitchen accessories, storage and gardening. There is also a range of electrical goods. The store will move with the seasons so will be a one stop shop for your Christmas accessories also.

Joyce's Supermarkets in Knocknacarra and Tuam also have a homestore section contained within the main supermarket

Catering


Joyce's Supermarkets can cater for any event, large or small. We like to share our passion for food by bringing you new tastes from around the world, which compliment the very best of local produce.

Our dishes are freshly prepared by our team of in-house chefs with the highest quality ingredients and delivered to your door. Our guarantee is to cater for each event with the professionalism and passion that you are your guests deserve.

Wines


Joyce's Supermarkets has built a strong reputation of supplying quality, fine tasting wines from around the world at a great price. We work with a variety of suppliers to offer our customers the best possible choice from well-known brands to wines from more select vineyards. Our off licence staff are constantly updating our range by adding new and exciting wines and delisting others when they have peaked. We continuously seek value for money and always look to secure the best possible offers to pass on to our customers.

There really is a wine to suit every taste and pocket. Our enthusiastic staff are delighted to offer advice on your selection. We also have a selection of craft brewed and natural beers from around the world.

Fresh Food


High quality fresh food is a key part of daily healthy living. We offer a wide variety of fresh foods at the best possible prices and place great emphasis on maintaining the highest standards of our fresh food range including fruit and vegetables, sandwiches, hot and cold delicatessen fooods and our prepared meals. All our suppliers are carefully chosen to bring you the best quality fresh foods and all our meats are traced from farm to fork.
